IAN CLESTER BRINGAS

Meet the author of Taekwondo Guide by Clester, Ian Clester Bringas. A dedicated martial artist who holds a second-dan Taekwondo black belt. Ian has immersed himself in the discipline, embodying its principles of perseverance, integrity, and indomitable spirit, driven by a transcendent passion for the art. Ian has honed his skills through years of rigorous training and unwavering commitment, achieving mastery of Taekwondo's intricate techniques and profound philosophy. As a second dan black belt, Ian not only embodies the essence of Taekwondo, but also serves as an inspiration to fellow practitioners, demonstrating martial arts' transformative power on and off the mat.
